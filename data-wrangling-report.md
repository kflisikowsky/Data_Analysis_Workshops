## 3. Key Findings

### Ex1: Occupation Analysis (Steps 1-18)
In this section, we established the statistical profile of the user base. Key operations included setting custom indexes and identifying demographic trends, such as the most frequent occupations and the average age within the population.

### Ex2: Euro 2012 Statistics (Steps 1-14)
The football dataset was used to practice complex filtering. We successfully isolated teams based on specific performance criteria (e.g., goals scored, shooting accuracy) and compared disciplinary records (Yellow and Red cards) across different nations.

### Ex3: Alcohol Consumption (Steps 1-8)
The final part of the project focused on data aggregation. Using the `groupby` function, we calculated the average consumption of beer, wine, and spirits per continent, identifying significant regional trends in alcohol preferences.
